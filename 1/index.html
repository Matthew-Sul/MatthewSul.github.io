Omnipotent Overview

The project’s primary objective is to use the digitized versions of red, green and blue (image) channels to align, stack and restore the image in full color using image processing methods. For all methods, I aligned the red and green channels to the blue one (as my baseline).

Basic Baseline (align image with no image processing methods)

Using the headbutt throw approach and not processing them in any meaningful way leads to ghosting and blurry images.

Sweet Summer Child Method (Exhaustive Search)

Aligned the images as best as I can by translating (used np.roll) images with varying offsets. The metric working best for me among L2 Norm, NCC and SSD was SSD. Since the .tif files were huge and exhaustively searching the entire thing would go at the speed of old people Mystic, elected to use the recommended [-15,15] range from the spec (albeit it still takes quite a bit). Cropped all the edges by 8% to elim most border discrepancies before processing with exhaustive search.

The Evolution (Image Pyramid)

As the .tifs are huge, utilized Image Pyramid methodology to downscale the given image down by a factor of 2 up to the point where the resolution was less than 32. Used exhaustive search on the small ones and kept shifting and rescaling up by 2 as appropriate to take less time and have less area to search for on the larger resolutions (from OH). Still takes quite awhile to run tho albeit not as egregious as kiwi on pizza.

Mega-Evolution (Bells and Whistles, imo whistles are better as can’t use our bodies to bell effectively tho we can whistle)

Using Image Pyramid drastically improved most images to look decent tho still had some discrepancies, in particular Emir. Used automatic cropping/edge detection with Sobel to do so. Images look better but still runs at the speed of beans. Eliminated the side discolorations for the most part so image looks cleaner than before

Emir went from having a red ghostly parasitic twin to looking like a fancy elegant regular dude and most other images got slightly less blurry
